GPs Near You and other useful links

General practitioners are usually the first medical expert a patient sees and as mentioned previously, are there to check all of you.
They take a holistic approach on patients and play an important role in getting them the treatment they need.
You'll work in a team with other healthcare professionals to discuss care options for patients and their families so teamwork is a necessary part of the GPs working life
GPs also need to keep up with new drugs being made and have to discuss the development of new pharmaceutical products with pharmaceutical sales representatives
